APP_NAME: Marrakech Guide Offline
SHORT_DESCRIPTION: Prices, phrases, and navigation for Marrakech. Works fully offline.

FULL_DESCRIPTION:
The offline Marrakech guide you can trust.

Know what things should cost, avoid common tourist traps, and move through the city with confidence. Marrakech Guide Offline combines practical local guidance with clear in-the-moment tools so you can act calmly, even when you're offline.

What you can do:
- Check quoted prices instantly with Quote -> Action
- See expected MAD ranges and get polite counter-offer scripts
- Use Home Base compass to get back to your riad/hotel
- Browse curated places and practical tips (not overwhelming lists)
- Use a Darija phrasebook with transliteration for daily situations
- Follow itinerary route cards with next-stop guidance

Built natively for Android for reliable offline performance.
