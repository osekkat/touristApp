- User entering a taxi quote (150 MAD for airport ride)

- **Tablet 7"**: 1200 × 1920 pixels
- **Tablet 10"**: 1920 × 1200 pixels

## Visual Guidelines

### Text Overlays
- **Font:** System bold (SF Pro Bold / Roboto Bold)
- **Position:** Top of screenshot, centered
- **Background:** Semi-transparent terracotta gradient
- **Color:** White text on terracotta
- **Size:** 2-5 words maximum

### Color Palette
- Primary: Terracotta (#D4572B)
- Background overlay: rgba(212, 87, 43, 0.85)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF)

### Device Frames
- Use Apple/Google approved device frames OR
- Borderless/frameless (cleaner, modern look)

### Content Requirements
- Show realistic Marrakech content
- Use actual app data (not placeholder)
- Ensure text is readable at store thumbnail size
- No promotional stickers or badges

## Localization

Screenshots should be created in both languages:
- **English (en-US):** Primary, required
- **French (fr-FR):** Secondary, recommended for French market

For localized screenshots:
- Translate headline overlays
- Show app in corresponding language
- Keep same visual style
