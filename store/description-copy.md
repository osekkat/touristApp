### Full Description

**Navigate Marrakech like a local, even without internet.**

Marrakech Guide is your trusted companion for exploring Morocco's most vibrant city. Built for travelers who want genuine experiences—not tourist traps.

**Know Fair Prices**
Getting quoted 400 MAD for a taxi to Majorelle Garden? Check it instantly. Our price intelligence shows you what locals pay, what's fair for tourists, and when you're being overcharged. Get counter-offer suggestions that actually work.

**Navigate the Medina**
The ancient medina's winding alleys are easy to get lost in—and that's part of the charm. But when you're ready to head back, our offline compass always points home. No GPS signal? No problem. Set your riad as home base and find your way back, even in airplane mode.

**Speak the Language**
"Shukran" goes a long way. Learn essential Darija phrases with Arabic script and Latin transliteration for easy pronunciation. From haggling to thanking, connect with locals authentically.

**Curated, Not Overwhelming**
We don't list every restaurant and shop. We've personally vetted places worth your time—hidden riads, authentic souks, and local favorites. Quality over quantity.

**Works Completely Offline**
Download once on WiFi, then forget about data. Maps, prices, phrases, places—everything works in airplane mode. Perfect for international travelers avoiding roaming charges.

**Privacy First**
No accounts. No tracking. No data selling. Your location stays on your device. We're a paid app because we work for you, not advertisers.

**What's Included**
- 49 curated places with honest reviews
- 32 price cards covering common scenarios
- 28 essential Darija phrases with pronunciation
- Offline Medina map with home base compass
- Negotiation scripts for souks and taxis

Built by travelers, for travelers. Marrakech Guide is the app we wished existed on our first trip.
