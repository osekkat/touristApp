- **Time Out Marrakech**
  Strong for: curated “best of” lists (especially restaurants + major attractions) and quick recommendations. ([Time Out Worldwide][3])

- **My Guide Marrakech**
  Strong for: “handpicked” experiences, events, and places—often refreshed frequently. ([My Guide Marrakech][4])

## Quick way to use these resources (so you actually get “best rated” picks)

1. Use **Time Out** (or **Visit Marrakech**) to get a shortlist of “worth it” places. ([Time Out Worldwide][3])
2. Then verify with **Tripadvisor** ratings + read the **most recent** reviews (last 3–6 months). ([Tripadvisor][8])
3. For activities, compare **Airbnb Experiences** vs **GetYourGuide/Viator** and pick ones with **high review volume**. ([Airbnb][9])
4. For events, check **Guichet.com** or **events.ma**. ([guichet.com][14])
5. Save your final picks into a map/list (super helpful once you’re walking around).
